About Us

Welcome to Cross Connect, your hub for strengthening connections within the Christian Church community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enhance communication, foster collaboration, and build stronger relationships within the Christian Church community through innovative technology and compassionate service.

Our History

Founded in 2023, Cross Connect started with a vision to bring churches closer together in a digital age. Since then, we have grown into a vibrant community dedicated to supporting and connecting churches across the globe.

Our Values

We believe in the power of community, the importance of collaboration, and the impact of compassionate service. Our core values drive everything we do and guide our mission to support the Christian Church community.
